The-Vine Travel Guide

Site Name

The Vine Travel Guide — This site will showcase travel experiences, photography, and travel tips from various countries. I chose this name because it reflects a personal, authentic experience and makes it easy to remember.

Optional domain availability: thevinetravelguide.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to share beautiful travel stories, destination tips, and helpful planning resources for young travelers and students. The site will include destination guides, travel photos, packing checklists, and a contact form for questions or collaboration.

Scenarios

  • What are the best travel destinations for students on a budget?
  • Where can I find sample travel itineraries for weekend trips?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #006699 – Used for headings and navigation elements

Secondary Color: #f5f5f5 – Used for background and light sections

Typography

Headings: Arial, sans-serif

Body Text: Georgia, serif
